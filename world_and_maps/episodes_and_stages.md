# Cookie Run: World Map & Episodes

## EPN01: Escape from the Oven
**Description:** It's too hot! We need to get out of here!
The Cookies' great escape begins here.
Jump, slide, run and don't look back!

## EPN02: Primeval Forest
**Description:** Finally, the Cookie Kingdom! But it's all in ruins!
With a map for adventure in hands, you embark
on a journey to restore its former glory!

## EPN03: Dragon's Valley
**Description:** It's even hotter than inside the oven!
Make your way past the land of boiling
Lava. Watch out - the ground is shaking!

## EPN04: City of Wizards
**Description:** You've reached the City of Wizards in search for clues
about the Kingdom's fate. But the town is abandoned!
And strange objects block your path. Mysterious!

## EPN05: Dessert Paradise
**Description:** Following a sweet scent across the ocean, our Cookies found the Dessert Paradise - a land in the sky, flowing with the fountain of life! A mysterious and mighty presence is said to live at the top of the giant Tree of Life. Ready to find out?

## EPS01: Island of Coins
**Description:** The ghost of Pirate Cookie is surrounded by an eerie aura. That's because he's protecting his hidden treasures. Anything could happen on this island, so be careful.

## EPS02: Tower of Frozen Waves
**Description:** Far beyond the sea lies a mesmerizing tower that looks like frozen ocean waves. What secrets wait at the top? The higher you climb, the colder it gets. Don't get frozen in place!

## EPS03: Mysterious Rabbit Hole
**Description:** Once you step into the mysterious Marshmallow Hills, you'd better watch your footing around those rabbit holes. After all, there's no telling where they might lead!

## EPN06: Plum Blossom Palace
**Description:** At the entrance to an eerie, mystical village, suddenly a gate to the heavens opens up! A trail under blooming plum blossom trees leads... right into the netherworld?! Keep your wits here, or you might never find your way back!

## EPEV01: Coin Palace Rush
**Description:** A mysterious palace of gold appeared out of nowhere like a mirage! The Coin Palace sparkles with endless treasure and blinding golden glamour! Don't let all that gold fool you! Charge ahead with your eyes glued to the coins, and you're bound to run into trouble!

## EPEV02: XP-Elixir Workshop
**Description:** You discovered a secret alchemy workshop!
Grab the XP-Elixirs packed with Star Jellies
to grow your experience faster than ever!
